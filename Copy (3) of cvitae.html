Bio:

  • Born: Bologna, 1948

  • Education: Electronic Engineering, University of Bologna, 1973

  • 1974–1980: Teaching and scientific formation study grant, University of Bologna

  • 1980–1985: Research associate, University of Bologna

  • 1985–1990: Associate Professor of Operations Research, University of Bologna

  • 1990–1994: Full Professor of Operations Research and Management Science, University of Turin

  • 1994–: Full Professor of Operations Research, University of Bologna
